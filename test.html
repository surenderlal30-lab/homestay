Yatra Essentials & Etiquette

Ensure your pilgrimage is safe, respectful, and fulfilling by following these local guidelines.

What to Pack

  • Heavy woolens (even in summer)
  • Sturdy trekking shoes
  • Basic medical kit / altitude meds
  • Cash (ATMs are rare)
  • Valid Govt ID / Aadhaar
  • Raincoat / Umbrella

Temple Etiquette

1
Purity of Body and Mind

Bathe before visiting the temple. Consumption of alcohol and non-veg food is strictly prohibited in the village during Yatra.

2
Appropriate Attire

Wear modest clothing covering shoulders and knees. Head covering (chunni/cap) is recommended inside the sanctum.

3
Photography Restrictions

Respect signs regarding photography inside the main temple. Focus on inner devotion rather than digital capture.
